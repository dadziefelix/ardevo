ABOUT THIS DEMO

Ardevo is a real-time fraud detection engine built specifically for Ghana's mobile money ecosystem. Press RUN ANALYSIS and the system processes 100 MoMo transactions, a mix of random legitimate traffic and seeded fraud clusters, scoring each against 12 detection rules.

WHAT THE NUMBERS MEAN

Analyzed Total transactions processed this run.
Flagged Transactions scored MEDIUM or HIGH risk.
High Risk Transactions requiring immediate block or escalation.
Flag Rate Realistic Ghana MoMo fraud rate is 5 to 15%.

THE 12 DETECTION RULES

Recent reversal request · Repeat reversal offender · High transaction velocity · Unusual late night hour · Large transaction amount · Sub threshold structuring · High value USSD transfer · Fan out to multiple receivers · Agent account cash out · Cash in to immediate cash out · First time sender high value · Round number compounding

WHY ARDEVO

Ghana's MoMo fraud crisis
is growing. Ardevo was built to stop it.

Mobile money fraud is the fastest growing financial crime in West Africa. Existing tools are generic, expensive, and built for Western markets. Ardevo is purpose built for Ghana's MoMo ecosystem, detecting the specific attack patterns that generic tools miss.
