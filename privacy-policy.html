THE SHORT VERSION

We collect your name and email when you access the demo. We use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. We do not sell your data, share it with third parties, send unsolicited emails, or store any transaction data generated during the demo simulation. The demo runs entirely in your browser.

2. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect a small amount of information to operate the demo site. The table below summarizes exactly what we collect, how, why, and for how long.

DATA COLLECTED HOW WE COLLECT IT WHY WE COLLECT IT HOW LONG WE KEEP IT
Full name Demo access gate form To identify who is accessing the demo Until you request deletion

About the demo simulation: When you press RUN ANALYSIS, all transaction data is generated and processed entirely within your browser. No transaction data is sent to any server, stored anywhere, or associated with your identity in any way. The simulation is fully local to your device.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To maintain a record of individuals who have accessed the Ardevo demo, which helps us understand interest in the platform and prepare for commercial conversations.
  • To contact you in the future regarding Ardevo updates, pilot program opportunities, or commercial availability, if you have expressed interest. We will not contact you without a legitimate reason related to Ardevo.
  • To analyze site traffic and visitor behavior through Google Analytics so we can improve the demo experience.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, automated decision making, or any purpose unrelated to Ardevo.

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

Under Ghana's Data Protection Act 2012 and applicable international data protection principles, we process your personal information on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You provide your name and email voluntarily by completing the demo access form. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.
  • Legitimate interests: We have a legitimate interest in understanding who is engaging with the Ardevo platform as part of operating and developing the product.
5. HOW WE STORE YOUR INFORMATION

Your name and email address are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Felix Dadzie. Google Sheets is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's own privacy and security standards.

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we store, including restricting access to authorized personnel only. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. GOOGLE ANALYTICS

This site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics collects information about how visitors use the site, including pages visited, time spent on the site, and general device and browser information. This data is collected using cookies and similar tracking technologies.

Google Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized and is used solely to understand site traffic and improve the demo experience. We do not use Google Analytics data to identify individual visitors.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. The only parties who may access your information are:

  • Felix Dadzie, as the sole operator of Ardevo.
  • Google LLC, through the Google Sheets storage service and Google Analytics, subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from a competent authority.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Under Ghana's Data Protection Act 2012, you have the following rights:

  • Access: The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information. We will fulfill deletion requests within 14 days.
  • Withdrawal of consent: The right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Objection: The right to object to our processing where we rely on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact Felix Dadzie at dadziefelix@gmail.com. We will respond within 14 days.

9. COOKIES

The Ardevo demo site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. We do not use cookies to track your personal identity, serve advertising, or share your browsing behavior with third parties beyond Google Analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The Ardevo platform is intended for business and professional use only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.
